About Playmakers


PlayMakers is an innovative startup from India, founded on March 23, 2025, by two young visionaries – Aman Vishwas (14 years old) and Ashish Vishwas (10 years old).

Our mission is to bring children's and parents' ideas to life through customized game development.

At PlayMakers, we turn your imagination into reality! Whether it's a game featuring your favorite superheroes, your own name as the main character, or any other custom features you dream of, we make it happen. Simply share your ideas, and we’ll craft a unique game just for you.

Our pricing is flexible, starting at just ₹5, depending on the complexity of your game idea. Let’s create something amazing together!
Dream it. Play it. Make it with PlayMakers!
